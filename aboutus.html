ABOUT US

ADENIUMS FOR LIFE

Nursery– A platform for your all gardening needs
Gardens have been an indispensable part of our homes, more so now, ever than before – thanks to the need of the hour to create greener spaces around us and our wish to live close to nature.
Well, Gardening is not just about having a few plants in our backyard. It’s an art of cultivating plants, a passionate hobby for many, to curate different plants that add to the beauty of our homes.
NurseryLive, an online nursery that started its active operations in October 2014, is India’s largest online nursery, that has grown immensely in a short span of time, incurring high volume of sales and building a huge customer base.

Headquartered in vijayawada, this online market for gardening products sells more than 5000 stock keeping units (SKUs) of plants, seeds and garden accessories, all available at a single place.

Vision

  1. To make spaces around us green & healthy place to live.
  2. We believe in inspiring our customers to fulfill their destiny as enthusiastic plant nerds and observant naturalists.
  3. We cultivate our staff, clients, and neighbors to develop the ever more vital primordial bond between humans and plants.

Mission

  1. To develop an easy-to-use, affordable & quality driven platform that fulfills all gardening related needs across India.
  2. To be a respected source of healthy plants and useful coaching for urban gardeners, landscapers, and potential plant enthusiasts.
  3. To foster reverence for the natural world in Seattle's urban spaces.
  4. To be an empowered work place where employees are the main fuel for growth, change, and profitability.
  5. To be a productive member of the Fremont community, actively engaging with its denizens to promote our vision.

History

Tory Galloway started Piriformis in 1998 specializing in drought tolerant plants. It has been a nursery ever since and has seen four names and 5 owners. The new owner continues on with the name Urban Earth and picks up where the previous owner, Susan, left off. The nursery historically provides a diversity of drought tolerant plants, unusual plants, and plants that do well in our climate and are appropriate sizes for urban gardens.
